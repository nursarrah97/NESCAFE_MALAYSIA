    Where does coffee come from?

    Coffe is grown in more than 50 countries around "the coffee belt", in areas that have right combination of altitude, soil and wheather

    The Beans

    Coffee beans comes from the fruit called "cherries" of the coffee plant. The cherries are not good to eat but bean insides the cherries are full of flavour. Each variety of coffee plant has a different and distict flavour. There are more than 120 varieties, but most of the coffee we drink comes from either Arabica and Robusta, or a blend of the two.

    Why is coffee roasted?

    With the light roast will mild and toasted-grain taste, with a light body and plenty of acidity. With medium roast will more body, less acidity and can be the most fruity and sweet. With dark roast will the most intense and bitter , can have a smoky or chocolatey sweet taste

    About Us

    NESCAFE is brand of coffee made by NESTLE. The name is a portmanteau of the words "Nestle" and "Cafe"

    The World's Favourite Coffee

    When we first lauched NESCAFE in Switzerland on 1 April 1938, it was predicted to be huge success. But then World War II start in 1939, NESCAFE was a staple in the food rations of the US forces and they loved it. Quick, easy and delicious coffee.
    During the rest of the 1940s, the popularity of NESCAFE grew. Today, NESCAFE is enjoyed in over 180 countries and has become the world's favourite.

    On top of the World

    This photo was taken in 1953, during the record breaking expedition to Mount Everest with Edmund Hillary, Tenzing Norgay and trusty little tin of NESCAFE.

    To the moon and back

    Im 1969, NESCAFE joined the crew APOLLO 11, Neil Armstrong, Buzz Aldrin and Michael Collins in their pioneering mission. The first coffee land to land on moon.
